# Official Policies

Microbe Studio's official legal policies are maintained on the main website at `microbe-studio.com`. If there is any difference between product documentation and the official policy pages, the official website pages take precedence.

## Terms of Service

[Terms of Service](https://microbe-studio.com/terms) governs the use of Microbe Studio, including accounts, licenses, Tokens, Protected Flows, payments, acceptable use, intellectual property, termination, and limitation of liability.

This is the primary policy for understanding rights and obligations when using the desktop application, web portal, and related services.

## Privacy Policy

[Privacy Policy](https://microbe-studio.com/privacy) explains how Microbe Studio collects, uses, shares, retains, and protects user information.

It covers account information, purchase and Token wallet information, Paddle payment processing, device and license data, technical data, and security-related data.

## Refund Policy

[Refund Policy](https://microbe-studio.com/refund) explains refund eligibility for Tokens, License Packages, and Protected Flows.

Because Microbe Studio provides digital goods, a Token wallet, and digital entitlements, some items may not be eligible for refund after they have been activated, used, downloaded, or spent.

## Flow-Related Policy

For Flow ownership, licensing, and usage restrictions, see:

- [Flow Intellectual Property](./flow-intellectual-property)
- [Terms of Service](https://microbe-studio.com/terms)

The Terms of Service is the official governing document for use of the Service. The Flow Intellectual Property page in these docs is a supplementary explanation that helps Partners and Customers understand how Microbe Studio separates platform ownership from Flow ownership.
